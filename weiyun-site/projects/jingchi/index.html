為醫美龍頭集團打造的開單與營運系統

A tailor-made order & operations system for a leading aesthetics group

從 0 規劃龍頭醫美集團的開單與營運流程，透過需求訪談、功能優先級排序、可行性評估與跨團隊協作， 把高度客製的需求拆分為階段性交付方案，最終完成第三方串接與系統落地。

Scoped and shipped a 0 → 1 order & operations system for a leading med-aesthetics group. I turned highly customised requirements into staged deliverables — interviewing stakeholders, prioritising features, assessing feasibility, and coordinating third-party integrations to launch.

背景與挑戰

醫美龍頭集團長期仰賴人工作業，每月耗費超過 1,400 小時在重複性任務上。 他們需要一套 能對齊既有作業流程，又能標準化開單與營運的系統，同時在高度客製化需求下 維持交付節奏與法遵要求。

The client relied on manual workflows, burning 1,400+ hours a month on repetitive tasks. They needed a system that could respect their legacy flow while standardising orders & operations — all under heavy customisation and strict compliance constraints.

我的角色

  • 作為 PM 兼 導入 PM，負責從需求定義、產品規劃到導入落地的完整鏈路。
  • 協調客戶端、法遵、工程、營運四方利害關係人，確保每個階段目標對齊。
  • 主導功能優先級排序、可行性評估與第三方服務串接規劃。
  • Owned the end-to-end loop: discovery, product definition, delivery and on-site rollout.
  • Aligned four stakeholder groups — client, legal, engineering and operations — at every milestone.
  • Drove prioritisation, feasibility assessments and third-party integration planning.

流程與決策

  • 需求訪談：走訪客戶端門市，梳理開單、會員、排班、結算的端到端流程。
  • 需求分層：把客製需求拆成「核心必要 × 系統延伸 × 迭代優化」三層，分別對應 MVP、Phase 2、客製化模組。
  • 可行性評估：從技術、法遵、營運三個面向評估風險，建立替代方案。
  • 階段性交付：每個 phase 設定明確驗收條件與回饋迴圈，降低一次性交付的風險。
  • Discovery: on-site interviews across clinics to map order, membership, scheduling and billing flows end-to-end.
  • Stratified scoping: split customisation into Core / System Extension / Iteration — mapped to MVP, Phase 2 and bespoke modules.
  • Feasibility review: stress-tested each requirement against tech, legal and ops risk — with fallback plans.
  • Staged delivery: every phase had clear acceptance criteria and a feedback loop to de-risk big-bang releases.

成果與影響

系統順利落地後，成功為公司開啟醫美產業新市場線，成為後續醫美類客戶的標竿案。 對客戶而言，不再需要仰賴人工進行文件儲存與尋找，門市同仁得以把時間投入在真正產生價值的客戶服務上。

The launch opened a new vertical for the company in med-aesthetics, becoming the reference case for future clients. For the customer, clinic staff no longer wasted time filing and searching paperwork — they spent the reclaimed hours on what actually matters: serving patients.

我學到的事

  • 「高度客製」與「可規模化」可以並存，關鍵是把需求拆層，讓產品核心維持乾淨。
  • 法遵需求越早進入流程越好，等到導入後期才處理，成本會指數級上升。
  • 跨多方協作時，定期的「進度對齊會議」比大量的 email 更有效。
  • "Deep customisation" and "scalable product" can coexist — the trick is to stratify scope and keep the core clean.
  • Bring compliance into the room early. Cost of change grows exponentially the later it lands.
  • Across multiple stakeholder groups, recurring progress-alignment meetings beat long email threads every time.
