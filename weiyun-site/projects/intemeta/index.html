把客製的元宇宙系統，重構成 可複用的 SaaS

Turning a bespoke metaverse system into a repeatable SaaS

將公司原本一次性交付的元宇宙活動系統，透過優先級重構與模組化， 轉型為可複用的 SaaS 產品。上線時程縮短 25%，並促成與日本大型連鎖書店的跨國合作。

Refactored a one-off metaverse event system into a reusable SaaS product — rescoping priorities and modularising features. Launch time dropped 25% and unlocked a cross-border partnership with a major Japanese bookstore chain.

背景與挑戰

OSENSE 的元宇宙活動系統原本是「每個客戶都客製一次」的專案模式， 導致交付週期長、毛利低、難以擴張。我們需要在不中斷既有客戶交付的前提下， 把平台轉成 SaaS，讓同一套產品能同時服務多個客戶。

OSENSE's metaverse event platform ran on a "every client, a new fork" model — long cycles, thin margins, hard to scale. The goal: turn it into a SaaS that could serve multiple clients from a single codebase — without breaking existing deliveries.

我的角色

  • 主導 SaaS 化轉型的產品規劃與優先級重構。
  • 評估第三方金流（B2C Payment Flow）與 API 規格。
  • 建立產品文件庫（Sales Kit、操作手冊）與教育訓練流程。
  • 推動 Scrum + Asana 工作方法，提升團隊開發節奏與可預期性。
  • Owned the SaaS-ization roadmap and re-prioritised scope.
  • Vetted third-party payment providers and designed the B2C payment flow & API contract.
  • Built the product knowledge base — sales kits, ops playbooks and enablement training.
  • Introduced Scrum + Asana rituals to give the team a predictable rhythm.

流程與決策

  • 客戶訪談：盤點既有客戶共通需求，拆解出「平台功能 × 客戶專屬」兩層。
  • 優先級重構：把 roadmap 從「客戶驅動」轉向「產品平台驅動」，以共通需求優先。
  • 資訊同步機制：建立產品更新即時溝通流程，資訊同步時間 3 天 → 8 小時。
  • 商業化配套：規劃首套 B2C 金流系統，奠定產品商業化基礎。
  • Customer research: audited existing clients to split scope into Platform vs Client-specific.
  • Reprioritisation: shifted the roadmap from customer-driven to platform-driven, prioritising shared needs.
  • Sync ritual: built a real-time update pipeline — info sync time 3 days → 8 hours.
  • Monetisation: designed the first B2C payment flow to unlock commercialisation.

成果與影響

SaaS 化後，InteMeta 順利促成與日本大型連鎖書店的跨國合作， 為公司打開國際市場。產品文件庫與教育訓練流程的建立， 也讓業務與客服能在 PM 不介入的情況下獨立服務客戶，大幅提升拓展效率。

Post-SaaS-ization, InteMeta landed a cross-border partnership with a major Japanese bookstore chain, opening up international markets. With the new product library and enablement in place, sales and CS could run customer engagements end-to-end without needing PM escalation — making growth truly scalable.

我學到的事

  • SaaS 化的關鍵不是技術，而是說服團隊「放棄部分客製」。
  • 文件化不是交差，而是讓產品有辦法被「不是 PM 的人」賣出去。
  • 當工程與產品共用 Scrum 節奏，產品的可預期性會顯著提升。
  • The hardest part of SaaS-ization isn't technical — it's getting the team to let go of customisation.
  • Docs aren't a chore; they're what lets the product ship without the PM in the room.
  • Once engineering and product share a Scrum cadence, the roadmap becomes genuinely predictable.
