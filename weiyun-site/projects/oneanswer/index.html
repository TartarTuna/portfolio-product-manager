6 週把解題 APP 從 想法 變成 營收線

From idea to revenue line in 6 weeks

主導 OneAnswer 線上一對一解題 APP 從概念到上線的 0→1 歷程， 在 6 週內完成產品開發與大型連鎖補習班合作落地，後續獨立為集團全新營收來源。

Led the 0 → 1 delivery of OneAnswer — a 1-on-1 online tutoring app — from concept to launch in 6 weeks, shipping a partnership with a major tutoring chain that became a brand-new revenue line for the group.

背景與挑戰

線上教育市場高度競爭，學生在「當下不會」的瞬間，常常轉向免費工具而非付費平台。 集團希望切入「即問即答」的瞬時需求，同時透過 B2B2C 管道， 以最小成本驗證一個全新的營收線。挑戰是：如何在 6 週內同時完成產品、導師系統與合作方的對接。

Online learning is fiercely competitive, and in the "I'm stuck right now" moment, students often flip to free tools rather than paid platforms. The group wanted to tap that moment through a B2B2C channel — validating a brand-new revenue line with minimum cost. The constraint: ship a product, a tutor system and a partner integration, all in 6 weeks.

我的角色

  • 產品整體規劃：PRD、User Flow、媒合邏輯、結算流程。
  • 跨團隊對齊：與工程、設計、運營、合作方逐週 sync。
  • 導入 AI 工具：將教師解題流程自動化，提升處理量能。
  • Owned product definition — PRD, user flows, matching logic, settlement rules.
  • Ran weekly sync with engineering, design, ops and the channel partner.
  • Rolled out AI tooling into the tutor workflow to unlock more throughput.

流程與決策

  • Week 1：定義 MVP Scope — 只留下「發問 → 媒合 → 解題 → 結算」四個核心動作。
  • Week 2–4：與工程 pair 設計資料結構與媒合演算法；同步與合作方簽訂導流方案。
  • Week 5：封測，蒐集教師 + 學生雙端痛點。
  • Week 6：正式上線，上線後一週內迭代媒合邏輯與介面引導。
  • 上線後：引入 AI 解題輔助，提升教師單位時間可處理量。
  • Week 1: locked MVP to four verbs — ask → match → answer → settle.
  • Week 2–4: paired with engineers on data model & matching algorithm; closed partner funnel in parallel.
  • Week 5: closed beta, harvested pain points on both tutor & student sides.
  • Week 6: public launch; iterated matching logic and onboarding UX within the first week.
  • Post-launch: layered AI assistance into the tutor flow to increase throughput per tutor.

成果與影響

OneAnswer 成功與大型連鎖補習班達成長期合作，後續獨立為集團新產品線， 打開原本核心產品觸及不到的用戶市場。更關鍵的是，這個專案驗證了「6 週驗證新營收線」 在集團內是可複製的節奏。

OneAnswer locked in a long-term deal with a major tutoring chain and spun out as its own product line, reaching audiences the core product couldn't. Just as importantly, the sprint proved that "ship a new revenue line in 6 weeks" is a repeatable tempo inside the group.

我學到的事

  • 當時間是最大限制時，scope 必須先於功能 — 每一個「加一點點」都會拖長指數倍。
  • B2B2C 產品要同時照顧兩端用戶，媒合機制的設計決定了產品能否自己長大。
  • AI 工具的導入不是 replace 人，而是讓專業者做更多判斷、少做重複勞動。
  • When time is the biggest constraint, scope beats features — every "just one more" compounds exponentially.
  • B2B2C products serve two users at once; the matching layer decides whether the product can grow itself.
  • AI tooling isn't about replacing people — it's about letting experts judge more and grind less.
